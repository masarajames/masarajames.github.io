About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! As a 3rd year Computer Science student at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology, I am passionate about pursuing knowledge and hands-on skills in cyber security. I find the tech industry absolutely fascinating due to its dynamic nature and am always eager to explore new and upcoming technologies. Currently, I am honing my networking ,web penetration skills, analysing security threats and identifying potential vulnerabilities to strengthen my general cyber security capabilities. I am enthusiastic about taking on challenges and exploring the possibilities of cyber security. Beyond my technical expertise, I am an excellent communicator and team player. These skills have enabled me to work collaboratively with other individuals and teams towards achieving common goals. In the future, I am keen to take on more challenging projects and contribute to the development of innovative solutions in the cyber security industry. I am always willing to learn and grow, and I look forward to exploring the limitless possibilities that the tech industry has to offer.
