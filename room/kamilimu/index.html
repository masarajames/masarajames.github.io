My 8 months at Kamilimu

🎓 I have received my certificate after my graduation at KamiLimu !

🏆 What an amazing journey it has been! The 8 months at KamiLimu , I’ve experienced a transformation like never before. From a timid version of myself, I’ve blossomed into a courageous individual ready to take on any challenge that comes my way! 🚀.

One of the most remarkable accomplishments during this period was conquering my fear of public speaking.

Now, I can confidently stand in front of any crowd and deliver a compelling message without a hint of nervousness!

🎙️ Additionally, I’ve learned to embrace opportunities that I used to shy away from, and it has opened doors to remarkable experiences!But that’s not all!

🌟 I had the honour of collaborating with my teammates, sidonia simanto and Bethly Chemutai, and together, we built an Intrusion Detection and Prevention System (IDPS) - Fotress- that will help Saccos in Kenya reduce the risk of financial losses!

💻💡 The journey was full of challenges and triumphs, and I’m proud of what we achieved as a team!

However, the true highlight of this journey has been the amazing people I’ve met along the way who have been a constant source of inspiration and positivity! 🤝 A heartfelt thank you to my incredible friends, especially Mark Tanui, for their unwavering support, encouragement, and assistance throughout this remarkable journey! I am deeply thankful to all my mentors and fellow mentees who have inspired and motivated me throughout this incredible journey. The connections I’ve made here have become cherished friendships that I will carry with me for a lifetime. 🤗 As I step into this new chapter, I pledge to use my knowledge and abilities to contribute meaningfully to society and empower others to reach their fullest potential.
